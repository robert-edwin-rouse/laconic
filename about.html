Origin

Laconic was founded in 2025 by a pair of scientists in the the zoology department at the University of Cambridge. The goal was simple: we wanted to create a functional beverage using a Yerba mate base without synthetic additives and excessive sugar, which became our Iconic blend. Since then, we are continuing to grow, working on new flavours, the scientific basis, and our sustainability initiatives.
